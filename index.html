Tufts has a new food delivery service, powered entirely by students.

Order now

Who?

We are a group of students who signed up to do rotating pickup from Chipotle and other food places.

How?

There is a calendar that displays order times, and we try and figure out who is available at those times.

Why?

We recognize that most places don't deliver at absurd hours. We want you to enjoy your fish tacos at 2am without interrupting your studying.

How much?

We charge the cost of the order + 10% to deliver. An extra fee (undecided) applies for "rush" deliveries or times where nobody is available.
